Buttered Noodle

Description

You may be wondering, how difficult this ugly ass pasta could be and do I really need a recipe for a fucking pasta? Well, it's not hard at all, and all you need is two functional hands (besides ingredients of course). Recipe is here in case of you forget how to make a pasta.

Below this paragraph I'll provide the ingredients you're going to need.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package fettuccine noodles
  • 6 tablespoons butter, cut into pieces
  • â…“ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Let's get to Directions!

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in fettuccine, bring back to a boil, and cook pasta over medium heat until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and return pasta to pot.
  2. Mix butter, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper into pasta until evenly combined.
