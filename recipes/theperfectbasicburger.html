The Perfect Basic Hamburger

Description

Are you in need of a hamburger recipe that is basic and perfect at the same time? If so, that's the place you want to know about!

In this recipe, you're not only going to learn how to make a perfectly and simply made burgers, but also how it's efficient in daily basis. Because it's so simple!

First off, I'm going to share the ingredients in the following section.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Now, if we are all have the necessary ingredients, we can begin to make our hamburger.

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. Whisk together egg, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl. Add ground beef and bread crumbs and miw with your hands or a fork until well blended. Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties.
  3. Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degress C).
